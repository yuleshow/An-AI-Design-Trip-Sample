🚙 Pasadena ↔ Santa Clara Road Trip

2011 Jeep Wrangler Sahara · ~770 miles round trip · 2 days

🟦 Day 1 (Thu 4/9) — US-101 North 🟪 Day 2 (Fri 4/10) — CA-1 PCH South

📍 Day 1 (Thu 4/9) — Pasadena → Santa Clara via US-101

🏠 Pasadena — Departure

7:00 AM
Fill gas, grab coffee, take I-210 W → SR-134 W → US-101 N

🌊 Stop 1: Ventura

~8:30 AM · 70 mi · 1.5 hr
Ventura Pier & Promenade. First ocean views! Coffee & stretch.

🌴 Stop 2: Santa Barbara

~9:30 AM · 30 mi · 35 min
Stearns Wharf, State Street. The "American Riviera." Walk the wharf.

🏘️ Stop 3: Solvang — Lunch

~11:00 AM · 35 mi · 40 min
Danish village! Æbleskiver pancakes, windmills, bakeries. Lunch here.

⛪ Stop 4: San Luis Obispo

~1:15 PM · 75 mi · 1 hr 15 min
Mission SLO (1772), Bubblegum Alley, creek-side downtown stroll.
⛽ Refuel here

🍷 Stop 5: Paso Robles

~2:20 PM · 30 mi · 30 min
Wine country town square. Coffee, ice cream, stretch your legs.

🧄 Stop 6: Gilroy

~4:15 PM · 100 mi · 1 hr 40 min
"Garlic Capital of the World." Garlic ice cream, rest stop after long Salinas Valley drive.

🎓 Stop 7: Stanford University

~5:45–7:00 PM · 40 mi · 50 min
Palm Drive, Main Quad, Memorial Church (stunning at golden hour!), Hoover Tower. Free parking after 4 PM. 🌅 Sunset ~7:30 PM.

🏨 Hotel: Hyatt Place San Jose Airport

~7:15 PM · 15 mi · 20 min
1471 N 4th St, San Jose, CA 95112. $168.98/night. Free breakfast, Wi-Fi, pool. Check in & relax!

📍 Day 2 (Fri 4/10) — Santa Clara → Pasadena via CA-1 PCH 🌊

💼 Business Meeting — 3223 Kenneth St

9:00–11:00 AM
Two-hour business meeting. Breakfast at hotel, check out, drive to meeting by 8:45 AM.
💼 Business · 2 hours

🚗 Depart Santa Clara

~11:15 AM
Fill gas, then CA-17 S → CA-1 S toward Monterey. Long scenic day ahead!
⛽ Fill up before/after meeting

🦦 Stop 1: Monterey / Cannery Row

~1:00 PM · 75 mi · 1 hr 45 min
Cannery Row waterfront. Sea otters in the bay! Quick stop — lots of coast ahead.
⛽ LAST GAS for 90 miles!

🌲 Stop 2: 17-Mile Drive & Lone Cypress

~1:45 PM · 5 mi · $11.75 toll
Iconic 250+ year-old cypress on rocky outcrop. Bird Rock, Pebble Beach Golf Links, Ghost Tree. Stunning coastal drive!

🎨 Stop 3: Carmel-by-the-Sea

~2:30 PM · 5 mi · 10 min
100+ art galleries, storybook cottages, Carmel Mission (1770). No street addresses by design!

🌉 Stop 4: Bixby Bridge (Photo Stop)

~3:15 PM · 15 mi · 25 min
714-ft bridge, 280 ft above crashing surf. Most photographed bridge in CA. Quick photos from the pullout.

🦭 Stop 5: San Simeon — Elephant Seals

~5:30 PM · 60 mi · 2 hr
FREE boardwalk over thousands of elephant seals (up to 5,000 lbs!). Hearst Castle on the hilltop. 🌅 Golden hour!

👻 Stop 6: Harmony (Pop. 18)

~6:00 PM · 10 mi · 10 min
Ghost town! Old creamery (1869), glassworks, pottery. Photo with "Population 18" sign.

🪨 Stop 7: Morro Bay — Dinner

~6:20 PM · 15 mi · 20 min
Morro Rock — 581-ft volcanic plug. Quick seafood dinner on the Embarcadero. 🌅 Sunset ~7:30 PM.
⛽ Refuel after Big Sur

🏠 Arrive Home: Pasadena

~10:30 PM · 215 mi · 3.5 hr via US-101 S
SLO → Santa Barbara → Ventura → Pasadena. No traffic at this hour — smooth sailing home! 🎉
