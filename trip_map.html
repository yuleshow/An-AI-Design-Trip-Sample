📍 Day 1 (Thu 4/9) — Pasadena → Santa Clara via US-101

🏠 Pasadena — Departure

7:00 AM
Fill gas, grab coffee, take I-210 W → SR-134 W → US-101 N

🌊 Stop 1: Ventura

~8:30 AM · 70 mi · 1.5 hr
Ventura Pier & Promenade. First ocean views! Coffee & stretch.

🌴 Stop 2: Santa Barbara

~9:30 AM · 30 mi · 35 min
Stearns Wharf, State Street. The "American Riviera." Walk the wharf.

🏘️ Stop 3: Solvang

~10:30 AM · 35 mi · 40 min
Danish village! Æbleskiver pancakes, windmills, bakeries.

🏖️ Stop 4: Pismo Beach — Lunch

~12:00 PM · 60 mi · 1 hr
Splash Café clam chowder in a bread bowl — legendary! Walk the pier.
⛽ Refuel here

🍷 Stop 5: Paso Robles

~1:45 PM · 55 mi · 50 min
Wine country town square. Coffee, ice cream, stretch your legs.

🧄 Stop 6: Gilroy

~4:15 PM · 100 mi · 1 hr 45 min
"Garlic Capital of the World." Quick browse at Gilroy Premium Outlets.

💼 Arrive: 3223 Kenneth St, Santa Clara

~5:30 PM · 30 mi · 35 min
Drop off luggage at hotel, freshen up. Then head to Stanford!

🎓 Stop 7: Stanford University

~5:45–7:00 PM · 10 mi · 15 min
Palm Drive, Main Quad, Memorial Church (stunning at golden hour!), Hoover Tower. Free parking after 4 PM. Sunset ~7:40 PM — perfect dusk walk.

⚠️ CRITICAL — No gas stations for ~90 miles between Monterey and San Simeon!
Fill your tank completely in Monterey before entering Big Sur. No fuel, no cell service, winding cliffside roads.

📍 Day 2 (Fri 4/10) — Santa Clara → Pasadena via CA-1 PCH 🌊

💼 Business Meeting — 3223 Kenneth St

9:00–11:00 AM
Two-hour business meeting. Breakfast at hotel, check out, drive to meeting by 8:45 AM.
💼 Business · 2 hours

🚗 Depart Santa Clara

~11:15 AM
Fill gas, then CA-17 S → CA-1 S toward Monterey. Long scenic day ahead!
⛽ Fill up before/after meeting

🦦 Stop 1: Monterey / Cannery Row

~1:00 PM · 75 mi · 1 hr 45 min
Cannery Row waterfront. Sea otters in the bay! Quick stop — lots of coast ahead.
⛽ LAST GAS for 90 miles!

🌉 Stop 2: Bixby Bridge & Big Sur

~2:20 PM · 30 mi · 1 hr (winding!)
714-ft bridge, 280 ft above crashing surf. Most photographed bridge in CA. Jaw-dropping coastal cliffs.

🦭 Stop 3: San Simeon — Elephant Seals

~4:40 PM · 60 mi · 2 hr
FREE boardwalk over thousands of elephant seals (up to 5,000 lbs!). Hearst Castle on the hilltop.

🪨 Stop 4: Morro Bay — Dinner

~5:15 PM · 25 mi · 30 min
Morro Rock — 581-ft volcanic plug. Quick seafood dinner on the Embarcadero.
⛽ Refuel after Big Sur

🏠 Arrive Home: Pasadena

~9:15–9:30 PM · 215 mi · 3.5 hr via US-101 S
SLO → Santa Barbara → Ventura → Pasadena. No traffic at this hour — smooth sailing home! 🎉
